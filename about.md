I'm a software engineer who enjoys building products that make other developers more effective.

Lately I've been especially interested in how AI is changing software engineering—not just how we generate code, but how humans review it, understand it, and confidently ship it. Many of the projects and ideas you'll find here explore developer workflows, AI-assisted engineering, and ways to reduce unnecessary cognitive load.

Outside of work, I enjoy prototyping new ideas, writing about engineering, and exploring the intersection of software, design, and human-centered developer tools.
